Privacy Policy

```

Effective date: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sigma Octantis Studio handles information through this website and through our first product, First Answer.

Sigma Octantis Studio builds small digital tools for self-trust, agency, and better choices in an AI-saturated world. First Answer is a private place to write what you think before asking elsewhere.

Who we are

Sigma Octantis Studio is the studio responsible for this website and First Answer.

Contact: support@sigmaoctantis.app

What this policy covers

This policy covers:

  • the website at sigmaoctantis.app;
  • First Answer, our web app currently hosted at first-answer.lovable.app;
  • support or business contact by email.

If a future Sigma Octantis Studio product has different data practices, it may have its own privacy notice.

Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the website, provide First Answer, process subscriptions, respond to support requests, and keep the service secure.

Information you provide

  • Contact information: if you email us, we receive your email address and anything you choose to include in your message.
  • Account information: if you create a First Answer account, we collect the information needed to create and manage that account, such as your email address and authentication-related information.
  • First Answer card content: if you use First Answer, we store the content you choose to write, such as what you are trying to figure out, your first answer, optional fear, optional no-approval choice, optional small move, return answers, and related timestamps.
  • Subscription information: if you purchase a paid plan, we store information needed to recognize your subscription status, such as subscription status, price plan, customer identifiers, and renewal or cancellation information.

Information collected automatically

  • Technical information: our hosting and infrastructure providers may process information such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, access times, and server logs.
  • Local storage or cookies: the website or app may use browser storage or cookies for basic functionality, authentication, security, or remembering session-related information.

Payments

Paid subscriptions are processed by Stripe or another payment provider. We do not store your full payment card details on our own servers.

Payment providers may process information needed to complete the purchase, manage subscriptions, prevent fraud, issue invoices or receipts, and comply with legal obligations. Their handling of payment data is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

How we use information

We may use information to:

  • provide and maintain the website and First Answer;
  • create and manage user accounts;
  • save and display First Answer cards for the account that created them;
  • process subscriptions and manage access to paid features;
  • respond to support, privacy, business, or product questions;
  • protect the service from abuse, security issues, or technical problems;
  • understand whether the website or app is functioning properly;
  • comply with legal, tax, security, or operational obligations.

What we do not do

First Answer is designed to be private and narrow. We do not use the app to provide AI-generated answers, coaching, scoring, public sharing, social comparison, or interpretation of your entries.

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers.

Service providers

We use third-party service providers to operate the website and app. These providers may process information only as needed to provide their services to us.

These providers may include:

  • hosting and static website providers;
  • app-building and deployment providers;
  • database, authentication, and backend infrastructure providers;
  • payment processors;
  • email or support providers;
  • security, logging, or operational tools.

Data sharing

We may share information only in limited situations:

  • with service providers who help operate the website, app, payments, or support;
  • if required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable government request;
  • to protect the rights, safety, security, or integrity of users, the service, or others;
  • in connection with a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

First Answer card content is kept while your account is active or until it is deleted according to the tools and processes available in the app or by request, subject to technical, legal, security, or backup limitations.

Subscription and payment records may be retained for accounting, tax, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, and legal compliance purposes.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the website and app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for keeping your account login information secure and for using a trusted device and browser when accessing First Answer.

International processing

The providers we use may process information in countries other than the country where you live. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards or legal mechanisms for international transfers.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal information. These may include the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing.

You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, and the right not to be subject to certain automated decisions.

To make a privacy request, contact: support@sigmaoctantis.app

We may need to verify your identity before responding to a request.

Children

The website and First Answer are not intended for children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will review the request.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and may provide additional notice where appropriate.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: support@sigmaoctantis.app
