Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026

Sigma Octantis is an early-stage independent studio developing meaningful micro-games, mobile tools, and personal growth experiences for iOS, Android, and the web.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect through this website and how we use it. Our first products are currently in development and are not yet publicly available.

Information we collect

At this time, this website does not offer user accounts, public app downloads, paid products, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. We may collect limited information in the following situations:

  • If you contact us by email, we collect your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.
  • Our hosting provider may process basic technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device information, access times, and server logs, to operate, secure, and maintain the website.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries.
  • To operate, maintain, and secure this website.
  • To prepare for future product launches.
  • To understand whether the website is functioning properly.
  • To comply with legal, security, or operational obligations.

Payments

We do not currently process payments through this website. If we offer paid apps, subscriptions, in-app purchases, or digital services in the future, payment processing may be handled by third-party providers such as Stripe, Apple, Google, or other payment platforms.

Future products

Future Sigma Octantis products may have their own privacy notices depending on the features, platforms, analytics tools, accounts, payment methods, or user data involved. Product-specific privacy information will be provided before or at launch where required.

Third-party services

This website may rely on third-party providers for domain registration, hosting, email, security, and future payment processing. These providers may process limited information as needed to provide their services.

Children’s privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website. Future apps or games will include age-appropriate privacy information where required.

Data retention

If you contact us by email, we may retain your message for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain business records, or comply with legal obligations.

Your choices

You may contact us to ask questions about information you have provided to us or to request that we delete a prior email inquiry where legally and operationally possible.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our website, products, or services evolve. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact
