Meaningful micro-games and mobile tools

Small digital experiences for self-trust, personal growth, and better choices.

Sigma Octantis is an early-stage independent studio creating mobile games and apps that help people build self-trust, make better choices, and grow through small real-world quests.

Our work is inspired by gamification, practical psychology, and personal progress — with an emphasis on becoming a better version of yourself rather than comparing yourself to others.

Short mobile games built around small quests, reflection, and personal progress.
